Our Indoor Kids Area

Being the proud parents of three little ones ourselves, we know and appreciate the difficulties and challenges of family outings with small children.

Therefore, it is our aim to offer a family-friendly experience to all parents/carers.

With dedicated indoor and outdoor playing areas and a great kids menu, The Royal Oak offers a relaxed and enjoyable dining experience for both the children and the adults, so you can bring your children along and still enjoy a few care-free hours, with a drink and/or a meal, while your kids are happily entertained.
